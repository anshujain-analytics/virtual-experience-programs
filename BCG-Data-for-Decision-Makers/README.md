# Boston Consulting Group (BCG X) – Data for Decision Makers

## 📌 Overview

Completed the BCG X Data for Decision Makers Virtual Experience Program through Forage.

The project focused on evaluating the effectiveness of multi-channel marketing campaigns and providing data-driven recommendations to increase new customer acquisition.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

NewCo conducted a one-week marketing campaign using two messaging styles across three marketing channels.

**Campaign A**
- Informal and conversational messaging

**Campaign B**
- Sales-focused promotional messaging

Channels used:

- Email
- Instagram
- Website Banner

The objective was to determine:

> Which campaign and marketing channel should the company invest in to maximize new customer growth?

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers
- Business Analysis

---

## 📊 Key Findings

### Overall Revenue

- Email generated the highest total revenue.
- Campaign B generated the highest overall sales.
- Email + Campaign B was the strongest combination for overall revenue.

### New Customer Analysis

After filtering only **New Customers**:

- Email + Campaign A generated the highest new customer sales.
- Informal messaging performed better than sales-focused messaging for acquiring first-time customers.

### Customer Behaviour

- Instagram recorded the highest average time on site for users who did not purchase.
- This audience can be targeted through future remarketing campaigns.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendation

Increase marketing investment in **Email + Campaign A** for customer acquisition because it generated the highest revenue from new customers.

Continue using **Campaign B** for existing customers to maximize overall revenue.
