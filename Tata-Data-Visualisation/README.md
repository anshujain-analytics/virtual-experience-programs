# Tata Group – Data Visualisation Virtual Experience

## 📌 Overview

Completed the Tata Group Data Visualisation Virtual Experience Program on Forage.

This project focused on transforming retail sales data into meaningful business insights through data cleaning, dashboard development, and executive reporting.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Clean retail transaction data
- Analyze revenue trends
- Identify top-performing countries
- Identify high-value customers
- Understand product demand across different markets
- Present insights for business decision-making

---

## 🛠 Skills Demonstrated

- Data Cleaning
- Data Analysis
- Data Visualization
- Business Intelligence
- Dashboard Design
- Executive Reporting
- Data Storytelling

---

## 📊 Key Business Insights

- Revenue increased significantly during the holiday season.
- Top international markets were identified outside the UK.
- High-value customers contributed a major share of revenue.
- Product demand analysis highlighted countries with strong expansion opportunities.
