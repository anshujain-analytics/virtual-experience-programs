# Task 1 – Understanding Client Sustainability Needs

## Business Problem

A large Australian university wanted to strengthen its sustainability initiatives and required consulting support to identify key ESG priorities and suitable sustainability solutions.

## Objectives

- Identify the client's top sustainability challenges
- Research industry best practices
- Analyze ESG tools and services
- Review sustainability regulations applicable to higher education

## Key Sustainability Issues Identified

- Curriculum Development
- Sustainable Buildings & Infrastructure
- Energy & Emissions
- Campus Engagement
- Industry Partnerships

### Deliverables

- Sustainability Issue Assessment
- Best Practices & ESG Tools
- Government & Business Sustainability Regulations

---

# Task 2 – Sustainability Gap Analysis

## Business Problem

Evaluate the client's current sustainability performance and identify areas requiring improvement.

## Analysis Performed

- Reviewed Sustainability Action Plan
- Compared sustainability performance with peer universities
- Identified organizational strengths and improvement areas
- Prioritized sustainability initiatives

### Key Findings

#### Areas Performing Well

- Environmental compliance
- Sustainability education initiatives
- Research and innovation partnerships
- Waste reduction programs

#### Areas for Improvement

- Climate adaptation and resilience
- Carbon neutrality
- Energy consumption
- Water conservation
- Sustainable infrastructure

### Business Recommendation

Prioritize initiatives focused on carbon reduction, sustainable infrastructure, and energy optimization while continuing investment in sustainability education and research collaborations.

# Task 3 – Comparative Analysis of ESG Solutions

## Business Problem

Evaluate available TCS sustainability solutions and determine which technologies best address the client's sustainability challenges.

## Solutions Compared

- TCS iON
- Circle4Life
- Clever Energy
- Zero Carbon Platform
- ESG Integration Solution

## Comparison Criteria

- ESG Reporting
- Energy Dashboard
- Energy Savings
- Emissions Measurement
- Real-time Monitoring
- IoT Integration
- Automated Data Collection
- Commercial Scalability

### Deliverable

Designed a comparative matrix highlighting the strengths and capabilities of each solution against the client's sustainability requirements.

# Task 4 – Client Value Proposition & Fitment Matrix

## Business Problem

Recommend the most appropriate sustainability solutions and present them in a consulting-style client presentation.

## Deliverables

- Client Fitment Matrix
- ESG Value Proposition
- Solution Recommendation
- Executive Presentation Slide

### Final Recommendation

Recommended a combination of:

- **TCS iON**
- **Clever Energy**
- **Zero Carbon Platform**

to address the client's primary sustainability priorities:

- Sustainable Buildings
- Climate Resilience
- Carbon Neutrality
- Energy Optimization
- Environmental Monitoring
